After 15+ years of developing software in a corporate environment, it's time to put hard-earned skills to use and build my own ideas.

As a developer, I've been a jack of all trades with a strong bias towards frontend. I have lead teams, I have architected solutions, but I have always returned to my passion - building cool things.

As a human, I value niceness and competence above all else. The world would be a much better place if we cared about others a little bit more and strived to be the best version of ourselves.


Shaped by too many science fiction novels, my current mental focus is figuring out what a human society looks like in the age of AI, what we need to change about how we interact and how we think in order to survive and thrive in the coming period.
